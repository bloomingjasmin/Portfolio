My short story

Hi, I’m Jasmin! I’m driven by one question: how can creativity create real impact? That question has followed me through Shanghai, Abu Dhabi, New York, and my hometown Tashkent, where I’ve worked on everything from community-centered research to brand storytelling and full marketing campaigns. My work usually lives at the intersection of strategy and emotion: part marketer, part researcher, part media-maker. I love digging into how people think, feel, and behave, then turning those insights into something that actually moves them: a campaign, a story, a product experience, a narrative that sticks.
